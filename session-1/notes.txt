SESSION 1 - "The Amber Road"
Date played: 2026-02-01
Players: 3

CHARACTERS
- Mira Ashveil — half-elf rogue, age ~28, from the Thornwood borderlands. Quiet, sharp-tongued, lost her brother to slavers years ago. Uses twin daggers. Player: Sarah.
- Aldric Sorn — human paladin of Solrath (sun god), age 34, former soldier. Big, idealistic, bad at lying. Carries a tower shield and longsword. Player: Marcus.
- Vexa Crankwheel — gnome artificer, age 71 (young for a gnome), obsessed with clockwork constructs. Carries a modified crossbow called "Sprocket." Player: Jamie.

SESSION SETUP / WHERE WE LEFT OFF
- First session, character introductions only.
- Party met in Dunholt, a small trade town on the Amber Road.

WHAT HAPPENED

Opening scene: The Pickled Goat tavern, Dunholt
- Party is separately hired by Innkeeper Bess Collow to look into the disappearances of three locals over the past month
- Missing: a miller's son (Tomas, 16), a merchant (unnamed), and a seamstress named Petra
- Bess says the town guard won't investigate — "too scared to go near the old mill at night"
- Each character gets 10 gold upfront, 40 on completion

Scene 2: Town exploration / investigation
- Aldric visits the garrison. Guard captain Renfew is dismissive and visibly nervous. Aldric rolls Insight — succeeds — senses Renfew is hiding something, possibly paid off or threatened.
- Mira scouts the docks. Finds a torn piece of cloth near the river with an unfamiliar symbol: a serpent eating its own tail, but with angular geometric cuts through it. She pockets it.
- Vexa visits the local alchemist, Dunby. He's agitated. Mentions that someone has been buying large quantities of sulfur and black salt — "enough to preserve a dozen bodies." He won't say who.

Scene 3: The Old Mill
- Party heads to the mill at dusk. Mill hasn't run in a year, officially "out of business."
- Mira climbs a tree and spots two figures entering the mill through a hidden cellar door.
- Party enters through the main doors (Aldric broke the lock with his shield — not subtle).
- Inside: rotten grain, rats, and a false floor in the back room. Vexa detects it with her tools.
- Beneath: stone steps leading down into a crudely carved tunnel smelling of sulfur.

Scene 4: The Tunnel
- Brief fight! Three cultists in plain gray robes, no symbols visible.
- Aldric tanks two of them. Mira sneak-attacks one from the shadows, drops him in two hits.
- Vexa uses Sprocket to fire a net-bolt — catches one cultist who tries to flee.
- Captured cultist refuses to talk. Aldric casts Zone of Truth — cultist is terrified but eventually says: "The Hollow needs the blood. The Sleeper must rise."
- Cultist passes out / pretends to pass out. Party ties him up.

Scene 5: Discovery
- Party finds a stone chamber at the end of the tunnel. Large ritual circle on the floor in dried blood.
- Three iron cages along the wall — two empty, one with a barely conscious young woman inside. NOT Petra (unknown identity).
- No bodies found. Unclear if the missing are dead or somewhere else.
- Giant bas-relief carved into the far wall: a dragon coiled around a mountain, one eye open.
- Aldric recognizes it as Vaeltharax, an ancient red dragon said to have been slain centuries ago by the Order of Solrath. His order has old texts about it.

SESSION ENDED HERE

CLIFFHANGERS / HOOKS FOR NEXT SESSION
- Who is the woman in the cage? She's conscious but won't speak.
- What is "the Hollow" and "the Sleeper"?
- The cultist is tied up — party needs to deal with him.
- Aldric's order may have information about Vaeltharax.
- Symbol Mira found — need to research it.

LOOT / XP

- Cultist dagger (plain, iron)
- Rolled-up parchment with partial map of something underground — unidentified location
- 120 XP each (exploration, combat, roleplay)

DM NOTES
- Renfew is on the cult's payroll. Not evil, just scared.
- The woman in the cage is Lena, a traveling scholar who got too close to the truth.
- Vaeltharax is not fully dead — entombed beneath the mountains to the north. Cult is trying to resurrect him through blood rituals.
- Vexa's player asked about crafting — need to work in a downtime crafting scene next session.
