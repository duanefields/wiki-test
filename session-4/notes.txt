SESSION 4 NOTES "The Hollow Road" Players: Mira, Aldric, Vexa DM notes in [brackets]

---

OPENING — Dunholt, morning after Renfew's unmasking

- Party still at Bess's inn. She's shaken, not sleeping. Makes them breakfast anyway. "What do you
  do with a town whose protector was a monster?"
- Aldric helps her board up the garrison office. Good roleplay moment, no roll needed.
- Lena shows up — she stayed the night, studying the medallion they took off Renfew
  - Medallion has a sigil she doesn't recognize but it REACTS when she holds it near the Mirrath map
    she's been carrying
  - "It's not a symbol of worship. It's a KEY of some kind."
- [DM NOTE: Lena is starting to realize she's in over her head. She's an academic. This is not what
  she signed up for. Foreshadowing her potential departure or a crisis point.]

---

SCENE 2 — The Hollow Road (travel scene)

- Party decides to follow the road southeast toward the Mirrath foothills. Renfew's papers mentioned
  a waystation — "the Cradle" — used by the Hollow Priests
- Vexa rigs a trip alarm for camp. Rolls 14 on tinker check. Works fine.
- Midday: encounter on road. Three travelers going the OTHER direction, panicked.
  - Pilgrims from a village called Ashenford
  - "Something drove us out. It was underground — sounds, like breathing, under the earth."
  - [DM NOTE: This is Vaeltharax stirring. Not waking, just dreaming. The dreams are bleeding into
    the surface world. Animals acting strange, ground cracking, fires starting spontaneously.]
  - Mira rolls Insight — 18 — she clocks that they're not lying but they're also not telling
    everything. One of them has a Hollow Priest brand on his forearm (hidden).
  - She doesn't confront yet. Tells Aldric quietly. He wants to stop them — Mira talks him down. "We
    learn more by letting them run."
  - Vexa quietly tracks them with a modified crossbow scope. Gets a direction of travel.

---

SCENE 3 — The Waystation ("the Cradle")

- Abandoned-looking farmhouse, but chimney is smoking
- Vexa scouts ahead (stealth roll: 17). Sees two guards in Hollow Priest robes, bored. One is
  picking at something on his boot. Second has a crossbow.
- Party plan: Aldric walks up openly as a "traveler." Vexa flanks. Mira goes rooftop.
  - Aldric rolls Persuasion: 9. Guards aren't buying the traveling merchant story.
  - Fight breaks out. Initiative order: Vexa, Guard 1, Mira, Aldric, Guard 2
  - Vexa: Sprocket fires — CRIT on guard with crossbow. He drops.
  - Guard 1 runs for the farmhouse door. Mira drops from roof (acrobatics 16), cuts him off with
    twin daggers. Rolls 11 to hit, 15 to hit. First misses (he dodges), second connects — 9 damage.
    Guard goes down on Aldric's follow-up (Divine Smite — Aldric has been holding that since the
    session started).

---

SCENE 4 — Inside the Cradle

- Farmhouse is a front. Trapdoor under a rug leads to a stone chamber below.
- Carvings on the walls — same sigil as Renfew's medallion. MORE complex here.
- [DM NOTE: This is a ritual prep space. The Hollow Priests have been using this location for
  months. The ritual isn't complete — Renfew's death disrupted the timeline. But it's close.]
- Find: logbook (partially burned — someone tried to destroy it)
  - Legible entries mention "the Third Calling," "the Sleeper responds," and a name: CORVAN.
  - Mira sees her brother's name. Hard stop. She goes very quiet.
  - Aldric notices. Rolls Insight — 20. He sees it. Puts his hand on her shoulder. Does not ask.
    Gives her a moment.
  - Vexa, oblivious, is already photographing everything with a small clockwork device. [Good Vexa
    moment — she's not cold, just focused]
- Lena translates a partial ritual inscription: "When the Sleeper tastes the blood of the Willing,
  the door opens from below."
  - "That means someone has to go in on purpose. A sacrifice that volunteers."

---

SCENE 5 — Camp, night

- Emotional scene. Mira finally tells the group about Corvan.
  - He disappeared 8 months ago. She thought he was dead. Seeing his name here — she doesn't know
    what to think. Is he a victim? A priest?
  - Aldric: "Whatever he became, that's not the end of his story. It never is."
  - Vexa: silence, then — "I'll build something that finds him. Give me two days." [Vexa is
    promising more than she can deliver and she knows it. Good arc moment.]
- Night watch. Aldric on first watch.
  - ENCOUNTER: a figure approaches from the treeline. Slow, arms out. NOT threatening.
  - It's a young woman, maybe 17. Ashenford refugee. Separated from the pilgrims.
  - Her name is SABLE. She has the same brand as the man on the road.
  - She says: "I didn't choose it. They said my sister would be safe."
  - [DM NOTE: Sable is a loose thread. She knows more about the Cradle than she's saying. Could be
    an asset or a liability. Leave this open.]
  - Aldric lets her stay. Makes her breakfast (rations). She sleeps by the fire.
