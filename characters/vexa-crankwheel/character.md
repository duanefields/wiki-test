# Vexa Crankwheel

**Class:** Artificer
**Race:** Gnome
**Level:** 3 (as of Session 3)
**Player:** Jamie

## Voice & Tone

Enthusiastic and technical. She keeps field notes, not a diary — she numbers her entries, records
observations methodically, and gets genuinely excited about mechanisms and discoveries. Her
emotional life shows up sideways: she notes something "interesting" where someone else would say
"frightening," or files a thing under "requires further research" when it has clearly gotten under
her skin.

Long view: she is 71 years old, young for a gnome, with perhaps 200 productive years ahead. She
holds a different scale of time than her companions, and it shows occasionally — especially when
thinking about Vaeltharax's patience.

**Tone:** curious, precise, delighted by problems

## Background

Gnome artificer, age 71. Obsessed with clockwork constructs and arcane mechanisms. Came to Dunholt
drawn by professional curiosity; stayed because the puzzle kept getting larger. She notices details
others miss. She is not helpless in a fight but thinks of combat primarily as a problem to be solved
efficiently so she can get back to the interesting part.

## Personal Arc

No single driving quest like Mira's, but deeply invested in understanding the Resonance Engine.
The Engine represents the most significant artificer discovery she has made in 47 years of
fieldwork. Decoding it is a long-term project. Her arc is intellectual as much as personal —
she is accumulating understanding that will matter when the party eventually confronts Vaeltharax
directly.
